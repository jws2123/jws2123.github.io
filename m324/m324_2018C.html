M324 A Advanced Multivariable Calculus

summer 2018

Course Summary

This course is a continuation of Math 126. The focus is mostly on integration in multiple variables. We discuss double and triple integrals; gradients and directional derivatives; line and surface integrals; and the theorems of Green, Stokes, and Gauss. In the second half of the course vector fields will play a key role -- the amazing rendering to the left (credit: Houdini Gibbons) is an example of such a vector field.
