Cryptography Worksheets for High School Students

The following notes and worksheets were developed for a math circle program targeting high school students in rural Indiana. Feel free to use/adapt! One note: the affine shift cypher worksheet was a bit challenging for the students, but turned out to be a good way to lead into the worksheets that followed (which I hoped to be more straightforward). Given time, these will eventually be tex'ed worksheets.

Lecture Notes

  • Session 1: Outline, Shift cipher worksheet, Shift cipher table, Anonymous voting technique example, Make your own cipher
  • Session 2: Affine Shift Ciphers
  • Lecture 3: Multiplication and Powers Worksheet
  • Lecture 4: Euler's Totient Function
  • Lecture 5: RSA
